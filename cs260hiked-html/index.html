    Top Trail

    The Timpanogos Cave trail is a moderately difficult 3.1 mile out-and-back trail. It terminates in the famous Timpanogos Cave. The trail has 1505 feet of elevation gain over 3.1 miles, so it is fairly steep. The entire cave is paved, so traction is not an issue. However, it occasionally has rock slides. Rangers are always present on the trail, and the traffic is relatively high. Highlights of the trail include beautiful canyon views of the American Fork Canyon and wildlife including various species of birds, mountain goats, and whitetail deer.
